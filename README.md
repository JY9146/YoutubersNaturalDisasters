We are finished! We're still updating the mod and making it more polished, but it is now released on CurseForge and Modrinth.

The mod requires the mods Photon and Weather Storms and Tornadoes (and their dependencies) 

Highly advise you get Skyfall: Meteors as well, it is a great mod that works really well with the rest of this mod.

Anyways here is my original really long description, gonna hopefully add a way to figure this out to put in survival.

You can turn off spawning with the disaster spawning gamerule, if you want to leave the chaos up to your friends.

The Features:

Disasters:

Meteors - They randomly fall from the sky, and when they land they create a circle of hot meteor, cold meteor, lava, and obsidian blocks. They can deal damage if they hit your head, so watch out! They can also be spawned by the Throw Meteor.

End Of World Meteor - Much larger version of the meteor, only spawnable by the End Of World Meteor button. (Try putting it in a jukebox). Don't spawn too many of these or they will crash your game.

Natural Gas - A green gas in a large radius which stays for about a minute, created by the Potion of Gas. It deals about half a heart of damage every tick (1/20th of a second), so try to get away as fast as you can!

Sinkhole - Picks random blocks to sink, with lava in tow! You might get flight or flight (or freeze). They delete bases in a instant. Spawned by the Sinkhole Summoner.

Earthquake - Has a small chance to appear in the world, shakes your screen and freezes you in place. That's pretty much it. It can also be started by a Earthquake Summoner.

Tornado - Weather Storms and Tornadoes F4 Tornado, spawned by the Tornado Summoner.

Volcano - Picks the most prominent block in a 40 block radius, and starts building up with it. Has lava balls which aim for players, and has a lot of lava spewing out. Messes up the ground as well.

Wildfire - A wildfire, where almost instantly a large circle of fire engulfs around the player.

Black Hole - A black hole which expands and eats up blocks and entities.

Timephoon - Time starts going crazy! At one moment your world can be going at 1/2 the regular tickrate, and the next it can be going 10 times faster.

Items:

Throw TNT - A button which causes an explostion, killing a player instantly. Doesn't damage the world however. In creative mode it's a jetpack. :)

Throw Lightning - A button which summons lightning where you right click.

Bred - The all powerful BRED, deals 9999 attack damage, never get's consumed, and gives more then enough saturation. Has a very small chance of dropping after murdering Knarfy.

Coin - A coin which you can flip.

Apple Of Wisdom - An apple given the power of Jchlatt, has a 50 50 chance of either giving you god buffs or smiting you out of existance.

Golden Apple Giver - Gives all players either a golden apple or an Apple of Wisdom, with a 50 50 chance.

Saturation Filler - Gives all players Saturation II for a few seconds.

Mace - Just a mace from the newer versions of Minecraft. Idk I was bored

Music Disc - A special music disc, referencing something from long ago. (Also gives you a special never before seen item!)

Technoblade - The most detailed NPC here, he has 200 hearts and diamond armor, with a diamond axe. He interacts with you when you right click (he can say around 67 different things in chat when you right click him). If you attack him he has a very small chance of insta killing you through multiple ways, such as ToothPick cleaning your teeth. You can trade with him with a Potion of Gas, a End Of World Meteor, or a Coin to get either an Apple of Wisdom, a Throw Meteor, a Throw TNT, or a Earthquake Summoner. You can also trade with him to temporarily forgive you if you attack him, unless you have damaged him by over 10 hearts. If you attack him he will play some boss music and start fighting, with multiple attack patterns. He will first attack you with his diamond axe, then use ender pearls to teleport around. He will then use a diamond sword, then shoot you with spectral arrows from a multishot crossbow (fireworks wouldn't work, sorry!). After that he will throw a Potion of Gas or two, and cause a explosions to try to get the jump on you. And then after that he will start floating and summon some minions (slightly weaker versions of Piglin Brutes), and Steve the Polar Bear to attack you (he won't come down until you defeat them all). Finally he will throw some potions on himself, giving him speed, strength, and resistance (all level I, don't worry!), and start the cycle again. Once he gets to 10 hearts, he puts on Netherite Armor and becomes invincible, whenever you attack him he will just say Technoblade Never Dies. You can only defeat him by a item provided by Orphan^2, who randomly spawns close to the battle once he become invincible. They will give you a representation of Techno's Old Videos, which kills him instantly. He drops his version of the End of World Meteor, which functions the same but does 20 damage when you use it against entities, one shotting a player. If he kills you however, he summons a Wither. This boss fight is intentional overpowered, in his honor.
